About The Author

My name is Birkir Brynjarsson. I played guitar and keyboard in Andrúm, and after the band split up I built this website as a school project in 2011 to preserve our music and photos.

Today I work as a programmer. You can find my code on GitHub and read more about what I do on my CV.
