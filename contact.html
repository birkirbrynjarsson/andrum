About The Author

My name is Birkir Brynjarsson and I am the creator and owner of this website. I joined Andrúm in the spring of 2005 and for the following 3 years, all (or at least most) of my energy and money went into developing and maintaining the band. It was my calling at the time.

Since the band quit I started working as a guitar teacher alongside school. I completed all the music theory I possible could at FÍH Music School where I was studying jazz-guitar and always planned to apply to the Academy of the Arts in Composition, but with growing interest in crafting things with my computer I quit taking private guitar lessons and began studying electronic music at Kópavogur Music School. And instead of going for composition at the Academy of the Arts, I applied into the Multimedia-school of Iceland, which I graduated from in the spring of 2011.

So my musical career has been pretty much on hold for a few years now. My initial thought when I was weighing my options between going for graphics and multimedia or music on the other hand was that it would be better to start with multimedia as it would come in handy when studying music later, rather than vice-versa… but then ehh…

Today I'm studying Computer Science in Sweden at the University of Skövde.

But that doesn't mean I've given up on music, I play "all the time", I'm even playing an instrument while making up this text. I'd love to release some music in the near future but… we'll see what time allows. Maybe I'm just looking for that perfect companion (a muse) to make music with, someone who has the same ambition and similar understanding and knowledge of music as myself.

Well,… As far as that goes at least I've been getting better at this design-thingy and I'm even learning some programming now. You're welcome to check out my portfolio, there you can find some of the things/work I've been doing for the past few years.
