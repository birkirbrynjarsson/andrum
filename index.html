Andrúm welcomes you!

This is the website for the Icelandic band Andrúm. Andrúm was active in the years 2003 - 2008, but quit soon after the release of their second full length album "Andvakar".

The purpose of the website is to preserve all or most of Andrúm's work and make it available to everybody.

You're welcome to take a look around the website, and please listen to some of the music, or stream "Andvakar" on Spotify.
